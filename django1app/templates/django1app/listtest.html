Origami:
Origami is the traditional Japanese art of paper folding, creating intricate shapes and designs by folding a single sheet of paper without cutting or gluing. It combines precision and creativity to produce beautiful three-dimensional figures and holds cultural significance in Japanese heritage.

Nested list
In HTML, a nested list refers to a list structure where one list (the inner list) is contained within another list (the outer list). This allows you to create hierarchical or indented lists, commonly used for creating sub-categories or related items within a main list, where each nested list item is further indented to visually represent the hierarchy.
